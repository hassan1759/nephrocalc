About NephroCalc

NephroCalc is designed to make kidney and dialysis-related calculations easier to access and understand. The goal is to provide clean, practical tools for education, clinical learning, and patient-friendly understanding.

These tools are intended to support learning and discussion. They should not replace individualized medical advice from a qualified healthcare professional.
